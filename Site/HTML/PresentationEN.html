Origins

The project's origin

The basic idea comes from David who thought that the Epita buildings in Villejuif would be a great place for the development of an horror game, especially in the middle of the night, because of the blue lights in the corridors which are pretty dark on the red walls. Once the team made, and since the idea seemed to please everybody, we adopted this as a base for the project. Each member then expressed his own vision of the game during an intense brain-storming. The game which resulted of the different view point's mix. We all agreed on the creation of a survival horror game. A First Person Shooter played inside Epita. C# being the programming language learned this year the game would be coded using it. And Unity being put at our disposal with the obligationto use, it goes without saying that the game would be made on Unity.

The team's origin

We are a group of four men. Simple, beautiful, intelligent and very curious. We are in the same class so we already know each other and we get along pretty well which will ease the cohesion of the group and will allow us to provide serious, precise, rigorous and painstaking work. Our love for video games put us together in order to fulfil our destiny : to create a game on UNITY. Even if the group formed pretty late, we are and will stay motivated so that we can carry out this ambitious project that you will discover.
